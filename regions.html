Check if ARBC is currently offered in your region!

ARBC is a regionally based competition so each team should register under their region first. If there are no in-person competitions in your area, the online qualifier is another opportunity for teams to qualify to the national competition! The date for the Online Qualifier is Sunday, January 21st, 2024. If your area doesn't have a competition, please register here.

For more information about a region, visit the region's page linked above or contact the corresponding regional director as shown in the About page.
