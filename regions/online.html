2025 Registration Form

Fill out this form to register for the 2025 ARBC Online Qualifier by Sunday, January 7th, 2024!

The 2024 Online Qualifiers will be held on Sunday, January 21st, 2024, from 1-6 pm EST. Teams of 3-4 students will be able to participate to compete for prizes and a spot at ARBC Nationals. There will be 4 rounds: individual, team, paper, and wave. Please register as soon as possible, we would love to see you there!
